INGREDIENTS

- 1/4 cup + 1 1/2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- Dash of salt
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vainilla extract
- 1 teaspoon sprinkles

HOW TO

1. STEP 1: Measure out the ingredients

2. STEP 2: Choose your favorite mug

3. STEP 3: Add flour, sugar, baking powder and salt

4. STEP 4: Stir the ingredients.

5. STEP 5: Stir in melted butter, vanilla, and finally the sprinkles

6. STEP 6: Microwave for 70-90 seconds and then allow cake to rest 1 min
